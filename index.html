Why Do We Protect Nature?

Bear Springs Blossom Nature Conservation is an international nonprofit NGO BSB supports nature preserves, some call them nature reserves. We try to protect beautiful Nature so the next generation of humans have a chance to survive! Bear Springs Blossom: The name comes from a bear, who was drinking out of a spring, surrounded by blossoms... BSB tries to explain life on Earth, shows ecological restoration. You get a feeling: What is Nature, and how conservation of Nature can save lives!
